Hi, thanks for stopping by! My name is Kelly Dern and I'm a Product Designer living in beautiful Boulder, Colorado.

I spend my days as a Product Designer for Life.io, a health and wellness company based out of Philadephia. Originally from the Pacific Northwest, I migrated to California to get a BA in Studio Art and Art History from Scripps College and then received my master's degree in Media and Communications from the London School of Economics and Political Sciences. Supporting the design community has always been a focus of mine; I've previously served on the Board of Directors of AIGA San Francisco and currently am a coding instructor with Girls Who Code. In a previous life, I worked in public relations with a focus in product promotions for global enterprise software companies. I'm also an illustrator and a painter.

I love collaborating, learning new tools and solving problems. If you're looking for a designer to collaborate, just want to chat or have a question for me, please get in touch at hello@kellydern.com.
