Hi, thanks for stopping by! My name is Kelly Dern and I'm a Product Designer living in beautiful Boulder, Colorado.

I spend my days as a Product Designer for Life.io, a lovely health and wellness company based out of Philadephia. I'm originally from the Pacific Northwest, and have a BA in Studio Art and Art History from Scripps College and received my master's degree in Media and Communications from the London School of Economics and Political Sciences.

I like to approach each design challenge with process-driven intentionality which is grounded in research and standardized design patterns to inform the identities and experiences that I create. My focus is to create work that drives actions and results.
